## Selected Awards, Honors, and Research Support

  *Strategic Research Award ($34,895)*

: **Texas A&M University, College of Education and Human Development**  
  *Graduate Research Award ($1,000)*  
  College-level award recognizing research potential and supporting an independent research project.
